# Phishing Email Sample

## Email Subject
Urgent: Your Bank Account Will Be Locked

## Sender Email
security-alert@hdfcbank-support.com

## Email Body (Summary)
The email claims that the user's bank account will be blocked and asks
the user to click a link to verify their account immediately.

## Red Flags Identified
- Urgent and threatening language
- Fake sender domain
- Suspicious link in email body
- Generic greeting

## SOC Analysis
This email matches common phishing patterns used to steal user credentials.

## Verdict
Confirmed Phishing Email
